### ðŸŒ The OSI Model (Open Systems Interconnection)
I reviewed the 7 layers that standardize network functions:
1. **Physical:** Transmission of raw bits over cables/fiber.
2. **Data Link:** MAC addressing and error detection (Frames).
3. **Network:** IP addressing and Routing (Packets).
4. **Transport:** End-to-end communication (TCP/UDP).
5. **Session:** Managing sessions between applications.
6. **Presentation:** Data encryption and formatting (SSL/TLS).
7. **Application:** Human-computer interaction (HTTP, FTP, SMTP).
